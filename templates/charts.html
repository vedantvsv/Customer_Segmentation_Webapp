Customer Segmentation & Churn Risk Distribution

📊 Segment Descriptions

  • Champions - Best customers, bought recently, often, and spend the most
  • Loyal - Frequent buyers with good recency
  • At Risk - Were good customers but haven't purchased recently
  • Hibernating - Low activity, haven't purchased in a while
  • Others - Customers not fitting other categories

⚠️ Risk Level Definitions

  • High Risk (>70%) - Very likely to churn, immediate action needed
  • Medium Risk (40-70%) - Should be monitored, consider retention offers
  • Low Risk (20-40%) - Relatively stable, keep engaged
  • Safe (<20%) - Low churn probability, focus on loyalty

💡 Business Actions

  • Champions - VIP rewards, early access to new products
  • At Risk - Win-back campaigns, personalized offers
  • Loyal - Loyalty programs, referral incentives
  • Hibernating - Re-engagement emails, deep discounts
